# WorldCup Edge Pro - Sales Demo Script

Use this for a 7-10 minute call.

## Opening

"This is not a betting bot and it does not guarantee profit. It is a market-discipline desk: it tells you the minimum price, suggested stake, and whether the bet is still playable."

## 60 Second Product Walkthrough

1. Open demo mode.
2. Show the Assistant.
3. Point to the key rule: do not play below minimum odds.
4. Open Book Finder.
5. Show Daily Betting Sheet.
6. Show Performance Lab and CLV.

## Key Line

"The product is not trying to make you bet more. It is trying to stop bad entries."

## Offer

"I am opening 20 private beta spots at 79 EUR for the World Cup build phase. You get private access, demo updates, daily sheets, bankroll controls, and performance tracking."

## Questions To Ask

- "Would this save you time during a matchday?"
- "Which screen would you actually open every day?"
- "Would 79 EUR feel cheap, fair, or expensive?"
- "What would stop you from paying today?"

## Close

If interested:
"I can reserve a beta spot and send payment/access instructions manually."

If unsure:
"No pressure. I will send the demo and the sample Daily Betting Sheet. Tell me which part feels useful and which part feels unnecessary."

## Do Not Say

- "This guarantees profit."
- "This beats bookmakers."
- "This places bets automatically."
- "You will win money."

## Say Instead

- "It tracks market value and discipline."
- "It helps avoid entering below the model's minimum price."
- "It measures CLV and bankroll risk over time."
- "It is analytics-only."
