FAQ

Does it place bets automatically?

No. It is an analytics desk. You place bets manually only with legal bookmakers available in your country.

Does it guarantee profit?

No. It measures value, price quality, risk and performance. Betting always involves losing money.

What makes it pro?

Assistant actions, minimum odds alerts, demo mode, daily reports, CLV tracking, bookmaker finder, bankroll controls, Top Plays ranking, and Performance Lab reporting.
