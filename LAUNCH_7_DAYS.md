# WorldCup Edge Pro - 7 Day Beta Launch

Goal: validate whether users will pay for a World Cup betting analytics desk before building cloud billing or an app-store package.

Positioning:
WorldCup Edge Pro is an analytics-only market desk for World Cup 2026. It helps users compare prices, respect minimum playable odds, size stakes, track CLV, and print a daily betting sheet. It does not guarantee profit, place bets, or handle bookmaker accounts.

Beta offer:
- Price: 79 EUR for the private World Cup beta pass.
- Includes: demo access, private dashboard, daily betting sheets, Book Finder, bankroll controls, CLV tracker, and build-phase updates.
- Limit: first 20 beta users.

Success target:
- 10 serious demos booked.
- 5 paid beta users or clear written intent to pay.
- At least 3 users say the Daily Betting Sheet is the core value.

## Day 1 - Package The Offer

- Finalize landing page message around "never enter below the minimum odds."
- Record a 60-second demo path:
  - open demo mode
  - show Assistant card
  - show quota minima
  - open Book Finder
  - open Daily Report
  - show Performance Lab
- Prepare a one-sentence offer:
  "Every matchday, WorldCup Edge Pro gives you a disciplined betting sheet with stake, minimum odds, best bookmaker, CLV tracking, and bankroll risk."

## Day 2 - Find First Testers

- DM 20 likely users:
  - betting friends
  - sports analytics people
  - tipsters
  - private betting communities
  - football creators
- Ask for a 10-minute demo, not a sale.
- Track answers in a simple sheet:
  name, channel, interested, objection, price reaction, follow-up date.

## Day 3 - Demo And Learn

- Run 5 live demos.
- Ask only three questions:
  - "Would this save you time during the World Cup?"
  - "Which screen would you use every matchday?"
  - "Would 79 EUR feel cheap, fair, or expensive?"
- Do not argue with objections. Capture exact words.

## Day 4 - Tighten Product

- Fix only issues repeated by at least two people.
- Improve the Daily Betting Sheet if users ask for clarity.
- Add missing bookmaker links only if they are in the target country.
- Keep the message analytics-only.

## Day 5 - Ask For Payment

- Return to warm users with:
  "I am opening 20 beta spots at 79 EUR. You get private access and daily sheets during the build phase. Want one?"
- Accept manual payment first.
- Do not build Stripe until someone tries to pay.

## Day 6 - Build Trust Assets

- Create:
  - 3 screenshots
  - 1 short demo video
  - 1 sample Daily Betting Sheet
  - clear disclaimer
  - FAQ for "does it guarantee profit?"
- Add any beta-user quotes only with permission.

## Day 7 - Decide

If 5+ people pay or strongly commit:
- Build cloud login and payment.
- Prepare customer account flow.
- Keep app-store plan paused.

If 1-4 people pay or commit:
- Continue private beta.
- Improve the offer and target a narrower audience.

If 0 people commit:
- Do not build more tech yet.
- Rework positioning and demo.

## Demo Script

1. "This is not a betting bot. It is a discipline layer."
2. "The key number is quota minima. Below this, we do not play."
3. "The Assistant tells you: playable, small only, or do not play."
4. "Book Finder shows where to check the best available price."
5. "Daily Report is the printable sheet for matchday."
6. "Performance Lab tells whether the model is beating the market over time."

## Objections

"Does it guarantee profit?"
No. It tracks value, price discipline, stake sizing, and CLV. Betting always has risk.

"Does it place bets?"
No. Users place bets manually only where legal for them.

"Why pay for it?"
Because it saves time, reduces emotional entries, tracks bankroll discipline, and makes market comparison easier.

"Why not just use Excel?"
Excel can calculate; this product turns calculation into a daily workflow with alerts, bookmaker checks, CLV, and reporting.
